What is Urungi

Unrungi is a self-service dashboard and reporting tool.
It has been written with the following ideas in mind:
  • easy to use for end-users
  • easy to use for reports creators
  • powerful for power-users
Urungi is a fork of the Widestage project: http://widestage.com

What does Urungi mean ?

Urungi is a Maori word meaning rudder, steering paddle, driver, navigator
http://maoridictionary.co.nz/word/8920
